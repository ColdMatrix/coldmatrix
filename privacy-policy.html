Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

ColdMatrix Tools ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use this website and how it's used.

Information We Collect

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site — such as which pages are viewed, general location (country/city level), and device type. This data is anonymized and aggregated; we do not collect names, emails, or personal identifiers unless you voluntarily provide them (for example, by contacting us).

How Calculator Inputs Are Used

Any values you enter into our calculators (room dimensions, panel specs, etc.) are processed entirely in your browser. We do not store, transmit, or have access to the specific numbers you enter into any calculator.

Cookies

Google Analytics may use cookies to distinguish visitors. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics — visitor traffic analysis
  • Netlify — website hosting

These providers have their own privacy policies governing the use of information they process.
